LOGO FILES NEEDED
==================

Add these files to this folder (/public):

1. logo.png or logo.svg
   - Main logo for navbar (light background)
   - Size: 160×40px recommended
   - Transparent background
   - Your brand color

2. logo-white.png or logo-white.svg
   - White version for footer (dark background)
   - Size: 160×40px recommended
   - Transparent background
   - Pure white color

QUICK START:
- If you don't have a logo yet, the site shows "SULTANIA" text
- Create logo at: https://www.canva.com/create/logos/
- Or use: https://www.namecheap.com/logo-maker/
- Save as PNG with transparent background
- Add files here

The website will automatically detect and use your logo!
