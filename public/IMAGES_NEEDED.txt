REQUIRED IMAGES FOR SEO
========================

Please create/add these images to the /public folder:

1. og-image.jpg (1200x630px)
   - Social media preview image
   - Shows when sharing on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
   - Should feature your brand logo and key products
   - Use high-quality, compressed JPEG

2. FAVICONS (use https://realfavicongenerator.net/)
   Upload your logo and it will generate all these:
   
   - favicon.ico (32x32)
   - favicon-16x16.png
   - favicon-32x32.png
   - apple-touch-icon.png (180x180)
   - android-chrome-192x192.png
   - android-chrome-512x512.png
   - mstile-150x150.png

HOW TO GENERATE FAVICONS:
1. Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net/
2. Upload your logo (square, at least 512x512px)
3. Customize colors if needed
4. Download the package
5. Extract all files to this /public folder
6. Done!
