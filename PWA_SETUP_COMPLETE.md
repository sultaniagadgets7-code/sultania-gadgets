## 🎨 Optional: Better Icons (Recommended)

For the best experience, create proper PWA icons:

### Required Sizes:
- **192x192** - Standard icon
- **512x512** - High-res icon
- **180x180** - Apple touch icon (already have this!)

### Design Tips:
- Use your logo on a solid background
- Keep important elements in the center 80%
- Test on both light and dark backgrounds
- Export as PNG with transparency

### Where to Create:
- Canva (free)
- Figma (free)
- Adobe Express (free)
- Or hire on Fiverr ($5-10)

### Once Created:
1. Save as `icon-192.png` and `icon-512.png`
2. Replace in `public/` folder
3. Update `site.webmanifest` to use new files
4. Deploy with `vercel --prod`
