PLACE YOUR FAVICON HERE
========================

Add your favicon.ico file to this folder:
sultania-gadgets/src/app/favicon.ico

QUICK STEPS:
1. Go to https://realfavicongenerator.net/
2. Upload your logo (lightning bolt)
3. Download generated files
4. Place favicon.ico HERE (in this folder)
5. Place other files in /public folder

MINIMUM REQUIRED:
- favicon.ico (32×32px) - goes HERE

RECOMMENDED:
- favicon.ico - HERE
- apple-icon.png - HERE
- Other sizes - in /public folder

The favicon will automatically appear in browser tabs!
